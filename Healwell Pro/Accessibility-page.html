Accessibility Commitment

Last Updated: January 2025

At Healwell, we are committed to making sure our website and services are accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We aim to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

1. Our Accessibility Features

  • Readable and consistent text styles.
  • High contrast design to improve visibility.
  • Alt-text on images for screen readers.
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation.
  • Clear headings and content structure.

2. Assistive Technologies Supported

Our site is tested with:

  • Screen readers (e.g., NVDA, JAWS).
  • Voice navigation software.
  • Keyboard-only navigation.
  • Zoom and text resizing tools.

3. Ongoing Improvements

We continue to review and enhance our website to improve accessibility. If you experience difficulty accessing any content, please contact us.
